Who are we?

We are Planet Promise, an organization with a mission to accomplish Sustainable Development Goals by 2030. The Sustainable Development Goals(SDGs) are 17 global goals set by the United Nations to end poverty, protect the planet, and ensure prosperity for all by 2030.They serve as a universal blueprint for achieving a sustainable and equitable future.We are trying to complete all goals one by one. we have already completed 8 goals and are currently at SDG9

What is SDG9?

SDG9:

Industry, Innovation and Infrastructure

SDG9 focuses on building resilient infrastructure and promoting sustainable industrialization. It encourages innovation to boost productivity and economic growth. The goal aims to make industries more inclusive, efficient, and environmentally friendly. By advancing technology and infrastructure, SDG 9 supports long-term development and global competitiveness.

Why SDG9 matters?

SDG 9 matters because it strengthens a nation’s backbone — its infrastructure and industries. It drives economic growth by creating jobs and improving productivity.It fuels innovation to create sustainable technology.By improving connectivity and technology, it empowers communities. Together, these ensure lasting development and build a resilient future for all

Our Goals

Through various steps can we say that SDG9 is fully achieved. Here are some ways we are using to accomplish SDG9:

1.Strengthen Infrastructure

  • Invest in resilient and sustainable infrastructure like clean energy grids, efficient transport systems,and water networks.
  • Focus on rural and underdeveloped areas to bridge the urban–rural infrastructure gap.
  • Use climate-resilient materials and designs to adapt to extreme weather and reduce environmental damage.

2. Promote Inclusive and Sustainable Industrialization

  • Support small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs) with access to finance, markets, and technology.
  • Encourage eco-friendly production and reduce industrial pollution through cleaner technologies.
  • Develop industrial policies that promote fair wages, safe working conditions, and gender equality.

3. Foster Innovation and Research

  • Increase investment in research and development (R&D) from both government and private sectors.
  • Build innovation hubs, incubators, and technology parks to support startups.
  • Strengthen partnerships between universities, industries, and governments for technology transfer.

4. Improve Access to Technology and Digitalization

  • Expand internet access and digital literacy, especially in developing regions.
  • Encourage digital transformation of industries using AI, robotics, and smart manufacturing.
  • Promote affordable and inclusive technologies that empower all communities.

5. Ensure Environmental Sustainability

  • Reduce the carbon footprint of industries through renewable energy and efficient processes.
  • Promote circular economy practices like recycling, reusing, and resource optimization.
  • Implement policies for sustainable resource management and emission control.
